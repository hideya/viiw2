Give your desktop background
a feel of depth...

Download

Precompiled and ZIP'ed (freeware)   for Mac OS X 10.10 or later

How to Run

  1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
  2. Right click on the app icon, then select "Open", if you encounter some security error when double-clicking the app when running it.
  3. To change the strength of the depth effect, please click the icon on the status bar at the top of the screen and use the slider.
