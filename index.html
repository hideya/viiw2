About

viiw2 is a little app that evolves your desktop experience by adding a feel of depth, which will give you more spacious and relaxing feel while using your Mac.

By running viiw2, a small status bar app, the current wallpaper gains extra dimension of the depth. viiw2 does that by moving the wallpaper image according to the mouse move. The image motion is specially designed so that it gives the best 3D feel in spite of the originally flat image. It includes carefully managed skewing that makes a huge difference in perception.

Hope you enjoy this newly added dimension to your desktop!

Download

Available for free at Mac App Store
or download Precompiled and ZIP'ed version
built for Mac OS X 10.10 or later

If you downloaded the zip, please see the following steps to run it:

  1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
  2. Right click on the app icon, then select "Open", if you encounter some security error when double-clicking the icon to run it.
  3. To change the strength of the depth effect or to quit, please click the icon on the status bar at the top of the screen.
